PARTICIPLE (V.ING)

Pattern

  1. ဝါကျရှေ့မှ Sub. ကိုဖြုတ်၍ Verb ကို "ing" သို့ပြောင်း။
  2. ဝါကျအလယ်မှ and ကိုဖြုတ်၍ ထိုနေရာမှာ Comma သုံး။
  3. Comma နောက်မှာ ရှေ့ဖြုတ်လိုက်သော Sub. ပြန်ထည့်။
  1. She had very good children and does not need to worry about anything.
    Having … … …
  2. He intended to buy a TV set and came down to Yangon.
    Intending … … …
  3. She has a very good memory and does not need to worry about passing the examination.
    Having … … …

Pattern

    Being … … …
  4. He came into the room when he saw me.
    Seeing … … …
  5. I left the room because I did not know what to do.
