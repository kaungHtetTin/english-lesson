TOO … TO … ENOUGH …

(i) too ကို adj ရှေ့ ၊ enough ကို adj နောက်၌ရေး။
(ii) ပြောင်းရာ၌ adj ကို ဆန့်ကျင့်ဘက်လုပ်။
(iii) တစ်ခုမှာ "not" ပါရင်၊ နောက်တစ်ခုမှာ "not" မပါ။
(1) The child is too young to go to school.
The child isn't … … …
(2) The chopper was too heavy for the old man to carry himself.
The chopper wasn't … … …
(3) He isn't thin enough to run fast.
He is too … … …
(4) U Ba was not rich enough to buy that house.
U Ba was too … … …
